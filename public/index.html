What We Deliver

Our Domain Controller helps developers, makers, and communities launch projects on the web in minutes.

  • Speed: Claim a custom subdomain on our BIND9 nameserver in seconds.
  • Control: Connect or swap IP targets without touching registrar dashboards.
  • Security: Keep ownership safe with password-only access to updates and deletion.
  • Reliability: Built on a self-hosted BIND9 infrastructure for maximum control.

How It Works

1. Check Availability

Enter your desired subdomain (e.g., "my-project"). We will instantly check to see if it's available.

2. Create Your Record

If available, enter your server's IPv4 address and a strong password. This password is your key to managing the record later.

3. Manage & Update

Need to change your IP? Just re-enter your subdomain, verify with your password, and update the IP or delete the record instantly.

Your new DNS record is written directly to our nameserver's zone file and reloaded. Global propagation usually begins within 60 seconds.
