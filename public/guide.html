Guide

Follow these steps to claim and manage your domain.

1. Check availability

Enter your preferred domain on the homepage. We validate the format and check every managed domain to confirm whether the name is available.

2. Create your record

Create an account, log in, and add the IPv4 address you want to point to. Records are written directly to our nameserver and loaded few minutes later.

3. Manage changes

Visit the dashboard whenever you need to update or delete a domain. Every change keeps your zone files in sync automatically.

Most DNS changes become visible worldwide within 5 minutes. If you need advanced settings, reach out through the Help page.
